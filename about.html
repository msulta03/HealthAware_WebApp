Now is the right time to be aware of your body.

Our Mission:

To empower and inspire you to live a healthier, more active life. We designed this web app to help you reach your fitness goals, whatever those may be. Health aware was started when our founders found themselves lost in the clutter that is health & fitnes.

As a junior in college, my health was an imaginary construct to me... I didn't really care about it, and all these damn apps that made me feel like shit weren't helping me either

Moe ,Health Aware Co-founder

We decided to create a simple health tracker! We will recommend a diet to follow and check in with you to see how you've hit your goals. HealthAware is the simple first step you need to take in order to create a healthier lifestyle - hence the name, Health aware.

Mind & Body in Tune

Stay in Tune, body and soul, through the HealthAware calorie tracker. Our community of heal enthusiasts that are looking for a quick way to stay healthy and aware of their bodies speak volumes on how health and fitness isn't, and should never be, too complicated

#EatHealthy

#EatHealthy is part of an online campaign we started to empower those who chose to meet their caloric daily goals. The hashtag isn't just for team HealthAware, but it's also for the larger fitness community, making it a great way to join the larger community and show off all our achievements!

Stay Active

HealthAware is the centeral hub for all things motivation! Our novice community are all united and excited towards the prospect of meeting their health goals. A large majority of the HealthAware community takes health awareness one step further by pursuing supplimentary physical activites. Look through our group forumns and find something you love to do!
